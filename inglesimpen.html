    How to improve

    Like we said before, English is something that can be learned with patience and time, and our patience will be rewarded when our future business goes forward.

    Next a series of tips that you can use to practice and improve your vocabulary and pronunciation:

    CORRECTION:

    Listen to your own pronunciation and if you don't find mistakes search for an native saying the same words. It doesn't have to be a real person, you can use videos to make it easy.

    TAKE IT SLOW:

    Start with sounds, then try with words and finish with full made sentences.

    IMAGINE:

    Think in how it pronouns, now think in the mouth 's position, the lips and the tongue.

    GIVE IT TIME:

    Focus on learning different sounds every day, that way learning how to pronounce more words constantly.

    DON'T BE SHY:

    Speak in front of a mirror and see the movement of your mouth when you pronounce some sounds.

    COPY:

    The best way to learn is by observing those who have English as their principal language, listen to radio programs, music, watch movies, series and documentaries without subtitles, watch how they move their lips and try to reply to it.

    TRY IT:

    Practice alone could make you lose the fear of speaking in public, and think in different situations.

    SEARCH FOR HELP:

    Find a partner with whom you could practice some words.

    READING:

    Practice poetry to improve intonation and pronunciation.

    MUSIC

    Sing in english, when you sing you improve your tone of voice and your rhythm. You can start with songs with a slow beat and simple lyrics, for example, we will rock you of Queen, is one of the most iconic songs of the band and too one of the favorites for those that teach the language, simple lyrics and simple beat make it a good option in the moment to learn, if you prefer, you can use children's songs if you see it easier.

    MINIMAL PAIRS

    Practice the “minimal pairs' ', these are words that are distinguished slightly for their pronunciation, they sound quite similar so it's easy to confuse them.

    HAVE A GOOD COMMAND OF IT:

    : Don't be afraid to articulate well, the fact of a good pronunciation shows your command of the language.

    EXPRESSIONS:

    Don't think in the grammar, at least not every moment, the natives of the language usually use expressions that cannot be traduced because are their own ones, likewise in Colombia have words that only are used in Colombia, in the moment to say it in other countries nobody will understand that you said, the better thing to do is learn someones of the expressions and look in what context you can use it.

    VOCABULARY:

    Prepare your vocabulary for different occasions, like make new friends, go to a restaurant, a work interview, etc.

    SAY IT:

    Tell anecdotes in English, this way you look for new vocabulary to tell your story in a more free way and make it sound interesting.

    ACCENTS:

    Practice the words with accent, sometimes even though they are the same word, the pronunciation can change its meaning.

    SPEAK MORE QUICKLY:

    The tongue-twisters are a good option in order to improve your speak velocity, and the pronunciation.

    Now you have all these tips in mind, only left to put in practice. The easier way to practice this and any other language is concentrate and focus only on that, it is just a discipline issue, not a talent issue.
