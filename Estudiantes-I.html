  • Benjaminist Student Profile

  • The school guides its processes to form a being:

  1. Committed to the Benjaminist mission and vision.
  2. Supportive, sensitive and collaborative.
  3. Honest, fair and upright.
  4. Reflective, critical, analytical and innovative reader of reality present.
  5. Respectful of self and others.
  6. Tolerant and able to function as a team, recognizing their capabilities and limitations, as well as those of others.
  7. Aware of your academic and technical training, which allows you connection to higher education and/or working life.
  8. Responsible and autonomous regarding your own life project.
  9. With a great sense of belonging, careful of their own resources those of the institution.
  10. With a high sense of appreciation and aesthetic judgment.
  11. Diligent in preserving the environment and caring for its around.
