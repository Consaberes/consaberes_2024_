Architectural design

Introduction

Architectural drawing is the way of expressing ideas through scale graphic representations, including the representation of buildings, landscapes and urban planning, following the standards of architectural design and regulations such as the NSR-10 (Earthquake Resistant Standard) and the standards of current graphics.

At the Benjamín Herrera Technical School, students of this specialty receive theoretical, technical and technological knowledge that allows them to develop both manual and digital graphic solutions, using various tools available on the market. In addition, students get involved in academic processes, which allows them to illustrate architectural elements through different graphic representations such as perspectives, models, orthogonal projections, sketches, computer designs, plans and 3D representations. These skills enable them to create realistic representations of architectural elements, materials, finishes and more, contributing to their training in this field.

Objective

Contribute effectively to the comprehensive training of technical students in architectural design, based on the study of topics focused on design, architecture and construction; based on the management of skills, specific knowledge and development of general work skills and citizenship skills.

Student profile

  • ♦ The student of the Architectural Design specialty is characterized by being responsible, creative, patient, dedicated to his work, capable of solving real problems with recursiveness and common sense and ethical attitude that is required to project it in your work, in the community and in your personal life and professional.

    • Occupation field

    The graduate of the Architectural Design specialty will be able to continue the professional training route in the following related disciplines: Architecture, architectural draftsman, graphic design, industrial design, fashion design, Bachelor's degree in design didactics, advertising, interior design and decoration , and engineering in general.
